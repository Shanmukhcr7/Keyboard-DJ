# Keyboard DJ 🎵 — Lofi Coding Beats

Turn your boring coding sessions into an interactive rhythm game! **Keyboard DJ** automatically plays your favorite background music (like Lofi hip-hop or upbeat EDM) *while* you type, and instantly pauses when you stop.

![Keyboard DJ Demo](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400.png?text=Interactive+Audio+in+VS+Code)

## Features

* **Interactive Audio**: The music responds to your typing! It pauses automatically if you stop typing for more than 300 milliseconds.
* **Focus & Productivity**: Designed specifically to keep you in the "flow state" without distracting you.
* **Cross-Platform**: Works directly inside VS Code on Windows, Mac, and Linux. No external software needed.
* **Custom Tracks**: (Coming soon) Swap out the default lo-fi track with your own!

## How to Use

1. Open the Command Palette (`Ctrl+Shift+P` or `Cmd+Shift+P`).
2. Type **Keyboard DJ: Start Music** and hit Enter.
3. Simply start typing in your editor and feel the music!
4. To stop the extension, run **Keyboard DJ: Stop Music**.

## Why Keyboard DJ?

Traditional music players play continuously, which can be distracting when you need to stop and think about a complex algorithm. Keyboard DJ acts as a metronome for your fingers—rewarding your active typing with music, and giving you silent focus when you pause to think.

## Requirements

No external dependencies required. 

## Known Issues
* If you close the hidden "Keyboard DJ Audio Engine" tab, the music will stop. Please leave the tab open in the background!
